Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Onibot?

Onibot starts with a conversation and ends with fully filled insurance forms. It is a friendly, jargon-free conversation that lives on your website, mobile phone. It can be a fullscreen conversation, a banner or a live chat like a widget. With a conversational user interface that has buttons, drop down menus, we are able to better match user expectations, increase engagement and finally improve conversion!

How does the onboarding work?

We will set up a 20-minute call to understand how your current website is built, your current workflow (what forms you use and the wholesalers you work with). Then we will set up a customized plan to embed Onibot on your website by inserting an HTML snippet. You can choose between three formats: Full page, Pop-up, Embed, Live chat.

How much coding knowledge is required?

Zero! We will hand hold you through the entire process and also advise on certain processes that can be automated.

Are there discounts for yearly plan?

Oh yeah! If you pay for a year in advance you save a 30% compared to paying per month. That means you get 12 months for less than the price of 10 months.

Do you do (insert insurance line of business)?

Yes, as long as there is form to be filled, a conversation to be created, we will make that happen for you!
